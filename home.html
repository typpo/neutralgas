How it works

A carbon offset is a reduction in emissions of carbon dioxide or greenhouse gases made in order to compensate for an emission made elsewhere.

Most people don't want to buy many tons of carbon offsets at a time. We purchase carbon offsets in bulk, lock in lower prices, and sell the rest in smaller chunks.

Accountability & Transparency

Neutral Gas is non-profit. We post records of all carbon purchases online in our Transparency Report and will notify customers when their micro-offset has been fulfilled in a larger bulk purchase. Bulk purchases occur at the end of each month.

All funds charged by Neutral Gas, less credit card fees, go to fund sustainable projects via the Carbon Neutral Company.

We support carbon-positive projects in places like Costa Rica and Ghana.
